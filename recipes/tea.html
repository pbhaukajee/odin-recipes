Tea

Description

Chai is easy to make and wonderful to enjoy. It is often enjoyed after a meal of Curry. This particular recipe, known as Chiah (pronounced "chee-ah") is one of many variations served in Southern Asia and some parts of Africa.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons black tea leaves (or 3 tea bags)
  • 3 cups boiling water
  • 2 cups milk, heated
  • 4 teaspoon sugar
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 2-3 cardamom pods, cracked open (or 1/2 tsp. ground cardamom)
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Steps

  1. Steep tea in boiling water.
  2. Add milk, sugar, cloves, cardamom and cinnamon stick.
  3. Simmer 10 minutes to blend flavors.
  4. Strain and serve hot.
